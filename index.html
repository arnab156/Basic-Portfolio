About Me

Arnab Majumdar has more than a decade of experience in corporate, academic and fashion industry. Arnab has a Masters degree in Textiles, Fashion Merchandising and Design from the University of Rhode Island.
Arnab is currently Assistant Professor at Northwood University and also owns a Chicago based small business called Made of Chicago.
